Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information

Last updated: January 2025

Back to Documentation

1. Information We Collect

GrokMarkets collects minimal personal information to provide our prediction market services:

  • Twitter Handle: Your Twitter username for market participation
  • Wallet Address: Solana address linked to your Twitter account
  • Trading Data: Market bets, winnings, and transaction history
  • Market Data: Prediction markets you create or participate in
  • Analytics: Website usage data via Google Analytics

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Process bets and payouts on prediction markets
  • Display market statistics and user activity
  • Resolve markets using Grok AI
  • Improve our platform and user experience
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • Public market data (anonymized betting amounts)
  • Legal compliance when required by law
  • Service providers (Twitter API, Solana network)

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS)
  • Secure database storage
  • Limited access to personal information
  • Regular security audits

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request data deletion
  • Opt-out of analytics tracking
  • Contact us with privacy concerns
