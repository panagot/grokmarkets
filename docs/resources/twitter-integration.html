Core Commands

Command Description Example
create Create a new prediction market @PANAGOT create "Will BTC hit $100K?"
yes/no [amount] Place a bet yes 2 or no 1.5
deposit Get your Solana deposit address @PANAGOT deposit
balance Check your current balance @PANAGOT balance
withdraw Withdraw SOL to your wallet @PANAGOT withdraw 5 [address]
help Get help and command list @PANAGOT help

Advanced Commands

For power users and market creators:

  • @PANAGOT stats - Your betting statistics
  • @PANAGOT leaderboard - Top winners
  • @PANAGOT trending - Popular markets
  • @PANAGOT challenge @user "question" - Challenge friends
  • @PANAGOT share [market_id] - Share a market
  • @PANAGOT template crypto/stocks/sports - Get templates

How @PANAGOT Works

@PANAGOT is our Twitter bot that handles all interactions. It:

  • ✅ Polls Twitter mentions every 15 minutes
  • ✅ Monitors Solana deposits every 1 minute
  • ✅ Checks expired markets every 10 minutes
  • ✅ Resolves markets via Grok API (99% reliable, <1 second)
  • ✅ Posts resolutions publicly on Twitter
  • ✅ Pays winners automatically

Fully automated 24/7! No manual intervention needed.
