Get Started on Twitter

GrokMarkets works entirely through Twitter! You don't need to connect any wallets or create accounts on the website.

1. Follow @PANAGOT on Twitter 2. Make sure your Twitter account is active 3. Start creating markets and betting via tweets 4. View live data on GrokMarkets.com
Tip: Your first deposit will automatically create a Solana address linked to your Twitter handle.
Important: All betting and market creation happens through Twitter replies and mentions. The website only shows data.

Place Your First Bet

Betting is done entirely through Twitter replies. Here's how:

  1. Find a market tweet from @PANAGOT
  2. Reply with "yes 1" or "no 2" (amount in SOL)
  3. @PANAGOT will process your bet
  4. View updated odds on GrokMarkets.com
Example: Betting 1.5 SOL on "YES" for Bitcoin $100K Reply: "yes 1.5" Current odds: 58.1% YES, 41.9% NO Potential payout: ~2.58 SOL if YES wins
Pro Tip: Start with small bets to understand the platform before making larger wagers.

Twitter Integration

All GrokMarkets interactions happen through Twitter commands with @PANAGOT:

Create Market: @PANAGOT create "Your prediction question?" Place Bet: Reply with "yes 1" or "no 2" (amount in SOL) Check Balance: @PANAGOT balance Withdraw: @PANAGOT withdraw 1 (amount in SOL) Deposit: Send SOL to your Twitter-linked address

Complete Twitter Command List:

  • @PANAGOT create "question?" - Create a new market
  • yes [amount] - Bet YES on a market (reply to tweet)
  • no [amount] - Bet NO on a market (reply to tweet)
  • @PANAGOT balance - Check your SOL balance
  • @PANAGOT withdraw [amount] - Withdraw SOL to your wallet
