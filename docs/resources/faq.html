Getting Started

What is GrokMarkets?
GrokMarkets is an AI-powered prediction market platform that operates entirely through Twitter. Users create markets, place bets, and interact via Twitter commands with @PANAGOT. The website only displays live market data. All transactions happen on Twitter and are secured by Solana blockchain.
Do I need to connect a wallet?
No wallet connection needed! GrokMarkets works entirely through Twitter. When you first deposit SOL or place a bet, a Solana address is automatically created and linked to your Twitter handle. You can withdraw to any Solana wallet later using @PANAGOT withdraw [amount].
How much SOL do I need to start?
You can start with as little as 0.1 SOL. Most bets range from 0.1 to 10 SOL. You can deposit SOL to your Twitter-linked address or start betting directly via Twitter replies. Start small to get familiar with the platform before making larger bets.

Markets & Betting

How do I create a market?
Creating a market is easy - just tweet @PANAGOT:
  • Tweet @PANAGOT create "Your prediction question?"
  • @PANAGOT will respond with market details and ID
  • Others can bet by replying to your tweet
  • View live data on GrokMarkets.com
Tip: Good market questions are specific, have clear deadlines, and binary outcomes (Yes/No).
How do I place a bet?
Betting is done entirely through Twitter replies:
  • Find a market tweet from @PANAGOT
  • Reply with your bet: "yes 1" or "no 2" (amount in SOL)
  • @PANAGOT processes your bet and updates odds
  • View live data on GrokMarkets.com
Note: The website only shows market data. All betting happens on Twitter.
How do odds work?
Odds represent the probability of an outcome. If YES has 60% odds, you have a 60% chance of winning. The payout is calculated as: Your Bet √ó (100 / Odds). So betting 1 SOL at 60% odds would pay ~1.67 SOL if you win.
When do I get my winnings?
Winnings are automatically credited to your Twitter-linked balance when Grok AI resolves the market. You can then withdraw them to any Solana wallet using @PANAGOT withdraw [amount] on Twitter. The withdrawal will be sent to your specified wallet address.

Twitter Integration

What is @PANAGOT?
@PANAGOT is the GrokMarkets Twitter bot that handles ALL platform interactions. It processes market creation, betting, balance checks, and withdrawals directly through Twitter replies and mentions. This is the ONLY way to interact with GrokMarkets - the website is purely for viewing data.
What Twitter commands can I use?
Here are ALL the Twitter commands you need:
@PANAGOT create "Your prediction question?" yes 1.5 (bet 1.5 SOL on YES - reply to market tweet) no 2 (bet 2 SOL on NO - reply to market tweet) @PANAGOT balance @PANAGOT withdraw 1.5
Important: These are the ONLY ways to interact with GrokMarkets. The website has no interactive features.
Do I need to connect my Twitter account?
No account connection needed! Just use the Twitter commands directly. The bot recognizes you by your Twitter handle (@username) and automatically creates a Solana address linked to it. Your first deposit or bet will establish this connection.

Grok AI Resolution

How does Grok AI resolve markets?
Grok AI automatically analyzes market outcomes using real-time data and context. It considers multiple factors like news, events, data sources, and verifiable information to make objective determinations. The AI provides reasoning for its decisions.
When are markets resolved?
Markets are resolved automatically when their end time is reached. Grok AI then analyzes the outcome and determines the winning side. This usually happens within minutes of the market's end time.
Can I dispute a resolution?
Resolutions by Grok AI are final and binding. The AI uses objective criteria and verifiable data sources. If you believe there's been an error, you can contact support, but resolutions are generally not reversible.

Security & Safety

Is my money safe?
Yes! GrokMarkets uses Solana smart contracts to secure all funds. Your money is held in escrow contracts until market resolution. We never have access to your private keys or funds.
Important: Never share your wallet's private keys or seed phrase with anyone. GrokMarkets will never ask for these.
What if I lose my wallet?
If you lose access to your wallet, you'll also lose access to your GrokMarkets balance and any unresolved bets. Always backup your wallet's seed phrase in a secure location.
